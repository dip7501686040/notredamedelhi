July – A Happening month

On the 12th of July, 2019 Notre Dame Staff and students celebrated St.Julie’s Feast. The institution of Notre Dame is marked by the charismatic spirit of St.Julie, which is simplicity of vision and joy in communicating to all people the goodness of God.

A special assembly was presented by the students of Std.VIII depicting the struggles of St.Julie and her unflinching faith in her maxim – “How good is the good God”.

On this auspicious day ‘Sweta Memorial Award’ was received by Agna Anto of Std IX A.

Sr.Mary Alice, enlightened the students with information on St.Julie. She also announced a competition ‘Know your roots’ to be held on 2nd Oct, 2019 in the school. She blessed the staff and the students in abundance and thanked each for being gifts as persons.

Farewell dear Stalwarts! 

 
"If you are brave enough to say good bye,

Life will reward you with a new HELLO!"
                                        - Paulo Coelho

On the 17th of May, 2019 Notre Dame students were seen buzzing around in vibrant costumes, beaming and bustling in the school arena….getting ready to bid farewell to their two sweethearts, Anshu Suri ma’am and Meena Gupta ma’am on their retirement.

Anshu ma’am served the institution for 27 years and Meena ma’am for 13 years. Both of them have been full of ideas, etiquette, disciplined and updated. Both were proficient teachers with qualities of good communication skills. Both actively participated in all curricular and extra curricular activities held in school.

A solemn prayer service with Ganesh Vandana and readings from scriptures was presented by the Student Council members with utmost gratitude and fervour.

An array of programmes were presented for the upliftment of the mood of one and all…. Everything possible from the milky galaxy of art and entertainment was encapsuled – skit , action song, dance-drama, songs, poem, medley, fairy tale etc.
Both the teachers were moved to tears of joy and pain. They thanked the Principal, Head Mistress and the students for their efforts.

Bouquets , blessings, prayers and gifts poured in , a sumptuous lunch was arranged in their honour.

Headmistress , Sister Mary Tanya and Principal Sister Mary Alice thanked them for being gifts as persons to the institution and the students. They blessed them and their family members in abundance.

Prevention is better than cure

On 15th April 2019, the C.I.S.F. team came to Notre Dame School to educate the students on fire safety rules. They delivered speeches on the various methods to prevent fire attacks.

Some of the methods were :-
* The fire fighting should not be started until hydrogen source is shut off.
* The vapour can be absorbed by using water. But water should never be used on liquid ammonia.
* Be sure that valves behind caps and plugs are tightly closed.
* To protect cylinders from physical damage; do not drag, roll, slide or drop it.

These ideas and methods of fire safety have greatly influenced Notre Damians. They will surely follow the safety measures at home and in school.

Thank you C.I.S.F. team for the enlightenment on the subject!

Farewell to Nirmala Maam

The fraternity of Notre Dame School bid farewell to Mrs.Nirmala Pant which was held on 22/02/2019 in the school premises.

An array of the programme was started with arti, prayer service by the school council and programmes like skit, action song, song, dance, folk-dance etc. All the programmes were performed beautifully under the able guidance of headmistress of the school Sr.Mary Tanya. The students of K.G. and primary participated and showed their talent through the programmes.

Mrs.Nirmala Pant worked in Notre Dame school Delhi for past nine years. She was a committed, punctual and a hardworking teacher. She gave her enormous contribution for the well being of the students. She is loved and appreciated by the students and the staff members of the school.

After the cultural programme mam thanked everyone for the love and affection she received from the students and the staff. She also shared her feelings , thoughts and expressions with one and all.

Exhibition of Projects done by students of Pre-Primary to Std.III

An exhibition was held on 01.02.2019 by the students of Pre-Primary and primary classes in Notre Dame School.

The exhibition was inaugurated by the Principal of the school Sr.Mary Alice. The exhibition was organised under the guidance of Sr.Mary Tanya, the Headmistress of the pre-primary and the primary sections of the school.


All the students of the Primary classes enthusiastically participated with the active support of their teachers. The projects of all the subjects viz; namely E.V.S., Hindi, English, Maths & Art and craft were displayed in a very attractive manner. All the exhibits were relevant in our day to day lives and thought provoking. The various projects gave new dimensions to the learners. Even the tiny tots of pre-primary classes actively participated with their colourful and beautiful projects.

The exhibition and the efforts of the students was appreciated by the students as well as the viewers.
