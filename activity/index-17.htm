Monthly Archives: August 2019

Staff Seminar

On 27th of July, 2019 a talk on how to be HAPPY TEACHERS was given by Sr.Mary Alice to all the staff members.

She emphasized that it is people and not programmes that determine the quality of Schools. teachers were encouraged to be positive, updated and impartial towards the students. Group discussion followed and resolutions to be implemented in the class were agreed upon.

This entry was posted in General on August 27, 2019 by ndsuser.

July – A Happening month

On the 12th of July, 2019 Notre Dame Staff and students celebrated St.Julie’s Feast. The institution of Notre Dame is marked by the charismatic spirit of St.Julie, which is simplicity of vision and joy in communicating to all people the goodness of God.

A special assembly was presented by the students of Std.VIII depicting the struggles of St.Julie and her unflinching faith in her maxim – “How good is the good God”.

On this auspicious day ‘Sweta Memorial Award’ was received by Agna Anto of Std IX A.

Sr.Mary Alice, enlightened the students with information on St.Julie. She also announced a competition ‘Know your roots’ to be held on 2nd Oct, 2019 in the school. She blessed the staff and the students in abundance and thanked each for being gifts as persons.
